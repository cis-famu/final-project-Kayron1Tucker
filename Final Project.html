About Kayron David Tucker

Kayron Tucker was born on September 19,2004 in Miami, Florida. He is the only and oldest boy amongst four younger sisters,along with being nurtured by a single mother in a low-income household. All his life he was raised to lead by example. His outlets were poetry, dance, track, and community service, which granted him many opportunities.

Portfolio

Throughout high school Kayron involved himself in multiple non-profits, clubs and sports. As a result he received multiple awards and titles, some include:

  • Valedictorian of his class
  • Mr. Achieve Miami
  • State Champions for Track and Field in the 800 and 4x800
  • Accumulated over $2.8 million in scholarships

    Kayron currently attends the #1 Public HBCU in the nation, Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University, as a first year civil engineering and international relations honors scholar on a BS/MS pathway.
